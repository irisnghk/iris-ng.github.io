About

Here's how my data journey begins

  • 2009 - 2010

    My Humble Beginnings

    Data mining on building query on banking systems, spreadsheet automation to facilitate calculation. A beginner's taste of business intelligence via Cognos. (Companies: AIA Hong Kong, HSBC)

  • 2010 - 2014

    A Data Lover is Born

    Performed data analytics and project management on customer segmentations and data automation for business campaign setups. Databases setting on MS Access and Excel automation, designed dashboard on Cognos with internal IT team on global banking group. (Company: HSBC)

  • 2014 - 2021

    Transition to Experienced Data Expert

    Delivered business insights to senior management and data-driven marketing. Advanced data analytics on SQL with integration to Oracle database, working on MS Azure cloud migration with IT team. Data visualisation on Power BI, more than 10 dashboard built. Promoted from Assistant Manager to Senior Manager in the period. (Company: AIA Hong Kong)

  • 2022 - 2023

    Regional Exposure

    Acted as Business Intelligence Lead to oversee BI projects across regions in Hong Kong, Singapore, Korea, Malaysia and China. Professional in Tableau and Power BI development. (Company: AIA Regional Office)
